Though Summer's here, back-to-school season is right around the corner, and for many kids, it's an exciting time.

But for the youth in our programs, it can also bring anxiety. Something as simple as not having a backpack or the supplies they need can make school overwhelming and stressful.

That's where you come in.

Each year, Kids Above All partners with individuals, community and faith groups, and businesses to step up so the 1,500 school-aged kids in our programs have the tools they need to start the year confident and ready to achieve their academic goals.
