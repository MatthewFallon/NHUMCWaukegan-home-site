## We are a [Reconciling Ministry](/statement) out of Waukegan, Illinois

We worship in person every Sunday at 10:00AM and also livestream from our [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/NewHopeUMCWaukegan).

Thank You for visiting the church of faith, love, and service!

Please stop by, we are located at [414 N. McAree Road in Waukegan, IL](https://goo.gl/maps/JmFrcFhzZWtCoZim6)

![A view of our altar setting.](/assets/images/altar-setting.jpg)

Please feel free to look through [Photos of Us](/gallery) to get a picture of us as a congregation.

We are a diverse congregation dedicated to answering the call of a loving God. God invites each of us to know God and serve each other. As we respond to this loving invitation our faith comes alive, our lives are transformed, and we find purpose and peace.
